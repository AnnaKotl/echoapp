Main Features of the Project

  • Visual Menu Browsing:

    Categorized dishes with rich visuals and details

  • Favorites:

    Users can save favorite items for quick reordering

  • Geolocation:

    Detects nearby restaurants and filters results by distance

  • Loyalty Program:

    Users track point balance,history, and rewards

  • Promotions & News:

    Live feed of seasonal offers and announcements

  • QR Scanner:

    Enables users to unlock offers or earn rewards in-store
